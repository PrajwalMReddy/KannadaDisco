# Past Simple Tense

### The past tense expresses actions that have already happened. As an example, ನೋಡ್ದೆ (nōḍde) means ‘I saw’. The relevant endings are given below.

## Singular Pronouns

| English | Kannada | Transcription |
|:-------:|:-------:|:-------------:|
|   I -   |   -ದೆ   |      -de      |
|  You -  |   -ದೆ   |      -de      |
|  He -   |   -ದ    |      -da      |
|  She -  |  -ದ್ಳು  |     -dḷu      |
|  It -   |  -ತ್ತು  |     -ttu      |

## Plural / Honorific Pronouns

| English | Kannada | Transcription |
|:-------:|:-------:|:-------------:|
|  We -   |  -ದ್ವಿ  |     -dvi      |
|  You -  |  -ದ್ರಿ  |     -dri      |
| They -  |  -ದ್ರು  |     -dru      |
